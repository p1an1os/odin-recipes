Hotdog Roll-Ups

Description

This sounds like a recipe for small Hot Dog's.

I've never tried by myself but please try it out it should be fine.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Beef hot dogs
  • Minced yellow onions
  • Sweet pickle relish
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Seasoned salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Using a rolling pin to do stuff
  3. Working with 1 slice of bread at a time
  4. Roll up tightly
  5. Stir together
  6. Serve hot dog roll ups
