Pizza

Description

This might be a nice pizza recipe which I also never tried.

Maybe you have time to try and feedback me your experience.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place warm water in a bowl
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough out
  6. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan
  7. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice
  8. Bake until ready
