Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for a lasagna which I never tried out but Odin told me to do this here.

It is probably a really good recipe which you should try out.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
    •

    Steps

    1. Make the Meat sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    4. Layer the Lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    5. Cover the foil and bake.
    6. Let the Lasagna rest before serving.
